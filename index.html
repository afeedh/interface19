About

What are we?

Interface is Kerala's largest student run startup accelarator that acts as a medium for the innovative minds to work with industry experts, and professionals through a two-day intensive mentoring programme where they will be introduced into the variable attributes of start-up ecosystem ranging from refining their pitch to fundraising. The teams winnowed after the mentorship programme will be invited to the final round that will be held during Tathva, where the teams will get to pitch their ideas before the Investors.
